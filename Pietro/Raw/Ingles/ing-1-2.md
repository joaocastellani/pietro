Capítulo 2 Urban accessibility
What is the topic? (transcribe the full introductory text)
Urban accessibility in my community Physical and social barriers affect people with disabilities a great deal since not having access to public spaces and means of transportation implies not being able to move around the city. This exclusion means fewer opportunities for education, employment and involvement in social policies. Have you ever thought about that?

Work in groups. Follow the instructions below and do some research about urban accessibility in your community. You can seek for information online or in the school library.a) Choose one of the topics below to research into. You can gather information about it from books, newspapers, online videos, social media posts etc. Make sure you indicate the source of information used. � The main barriers to accessibility for people with disabilities in public areas, buildings and transportation networks. How the authorities deal with complaints of the population regarding urban accessibility.b) Organize the data you have found in a visually attractive way to present it to the class. What were the most common aspects of accessibility discussed by the groups? Is there anything you can do to help those who suffer from the barriers of urban accessibility? Explain.
